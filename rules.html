MurderMobs Minecraft Server Rules

These are the rules for MurderMobs and the information for how to appeal a mute or ban.



Appealing

  If you believe you have been falsely muted or banned, please run the command '/msg Emmett1027 (The reason why you got banned) (duration of the ban) (Who banned you) (Why you think the ban was false)' in minehut, or message me; @Emmett1027 in github, with the same thing but with your username in Minecraft in the last part.



Chat Rules

  1. Please be respectful, and at least try restrain to words that aren't curses. Filter triggering 30 times in 1 hour when it's obviously a bad word will result in a 10 minute mute.

  2. Please do not be racist, please do not accuse somebody of racism if they defeat you somehow [1H MUTE], and please do not try to bypass the filter.

If you bypass the filter, it will result into a 1 hour ban.



Game Rules

  1. Please do not use any unfair modification that gives you an avantage over other players that you wouldn't have without it. If you believe you should be able to use it, please ask an admin or Emmett1027 himself. First offense will result into a 1 day ban, 2nd will result in a 10 day ban, 3rd will result into a 30 day ban, and 4th will result into a permanent ban.


  2. If you are an exploiter, and want to find exploits & all that good stuff, you can. You won't be banned for trying to find an exploit. Although, if you abuse it without telling a developer or the owner, that will result in a 5 day ban. If you tell a developer or staff or owner, you will be rewared, and you will be able to abuse the exploit or bug as much as you can without getting banned until a developer patches it.



General Rules

  1. Please do not annoy staff. It's rude, and if you do; you will get a mute for 10 minutes.


  2. If you have an argument with a staff and they are saying they will ban you because you broke the rules, but you think you didn't. Please tell a red ranked staff to decide this. If there are none on, just wait for them to log on.


Sorry for any inconviences in the rules or in appealing, if you have a suggestion, please suggest it in /suggest (suggestion) or message Emmett1027 about it.
